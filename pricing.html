Frequently Asked Questions

We offer everyday doorstep car cleaning using a process which helps keep your car looking like new throughout its life. Interior vacuuming is done using high suction powered vacuum once a week. Application of a top coat is done on the exterior painted panels which acts as a protective layer and prevents your paint from fading and other harmful elements.

The prices vary for different segment of cars depending on the size of car and complexity of panels. We have 4 segments for eg. Baleno( hatchback): 1300/- creta& ciaz( small suv/sedan): 1500/- fortuner/A4( medium suv/sedan): 1700/- Q7/A8( large suv/sedan): 1900/- For essential package.

We have 2 packages essential and premium. Premium package gives you another 2 vacuum cleaning along with application of conditioners on dashboard, leather and other surfaces.

The process of cleaning the cars, use of MFT's, vacuum cleaners, top coat application and efforts made by our car heros to maintain your paint shine and keep it in pristine condition contributes to our pricing. To deliver quality, consistency and great customer experience...the long term vision...everything sets the standards high and thus contribute to the pricing.

We work 6 days in a week and only Friday is our weekly off. Our car heros and we need a day to review all the things and also rest is very important to stay both mentally and physically fit.

We prefer working on Sundays as mostly everyone is at home and they get a chance to see the kind of work we are doing. We also get more time to get vacuuming and application of top coat on weekends.

We use MFT's with higher GSM as they are plush and have high absorption and is lint free....they help eliminate chances of scratching the vehicle. Multiple MFT's are used for every car which are washed everyday and clean towels are used whenever a car is washed.

Our vision is to help people realise the importance of cleaning the car with a proper process as it is the second most expensive thing after a house that he owns. It's a huge investment and properly maintaining your investment is a necessity not luxury. We are trying to give back to society by giving employment, save water and using battery operated scooter which has no CO2 emissions.

Apart from natural reasons, if incase we miss someday due to any reason we will refund your money for the same day.

We have backup car heros who knows all the locations and cars in a particular cluster. If anyone falls sick we won't take an off for the same reason.

Vacuum is done to clean the dirt and gunk on the seats, carpets, mats and boot. When it comes to deep cleaning your interiors then you have to get the drycleaning services which includes complete interiors detailing, however a spot somewhere on the seat or dashboard area will certainly be removed while vacuuming the car.

We start at 5am and we are there till 1pm... depending on your suitability we can fix a time window as we have time slots of one hour, if your preferred time window is available we can start immediately....else you have to make slight changes to the preferred time or wait for some more time till we start with another car hero at the same location.

We will fix a dedicated day for your car to get interiors done and also we do clean your car everyday if your car is unlocked so that the dirt and dust on the mats is cleaned properly.

We do use products made from different international brands which are best suited for car care services. 3M is one of the most recognised brand whose products are used in the process.

Sir our prices are completely fixed and we can't be flexible on the pricing as we are here with a long term plan and we couldnt have different pricing for different individuals...also the effort it takes to clean one car is same for another car.
