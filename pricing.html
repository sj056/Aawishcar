Frequently Asked Questions

We provide daily doorstep car cleaning using a process that keeps your car looking brand new for the duration of its life. Once a week, an interior vacuum with high suction power is used. On the exterior painted panels, a top coat is applied as a protective layer to shield the paint from the sun's rays and other harmful elements.

The prices vary for different segment of cars depending on the size of car and complexity of panels. Essential package pricing for these 4 segments are: Baleno( Hatchback): 1300/- Creta & Ciaz( Small Suv/Sedan): 1500/- Fortuner/ Audi A4( Medium Suv/Sedan): 1700/- Audi Q7/A8(Large Suv/sedan): 1900/-

The long-term goal of everything is to deliver quality, consistency, and a great customer experience. This raises the bar for everything and has an impact on pricing.

We work 6 days in a week and only Friday is our weekly off. Our car heros and we need a day to review all the things and also rest is very important to stay both mentally and physically fit.

Aside from natural causes, if we happen to miss a day for any reason, we'll refund your money for that particular day.

We start at 5am and stay until 1pm. Depending on your availability, we can set a time window; if your preferred time window is available, we can start right away. If not, you will need to make a few minor adjustments to your preferred time or wait until we start with another car hero at the same location.

We commit to making your car shine six days a week; our only day off is Friday. Therefore, in order to continuously improve our services, we take a day to review everything and getting enough rest is crucial to maintaining our physical and mental health.

We use MFTs with higher GSM because they are plush, have high absorption, and are lint-free, which reduces the likelihood that the vehicle will get scratched. Every day, each car is washed using multiple MFTs, and each time a car is washed, fresh towels are used.

In order to provide the best car care services, we do use products from various international brands. One of the most recognisable brands whose products are used in the process is 3M.

The dirt and grime on the seats, carpets, mats, and boot are cleaned with a vacuum. When it comes to thoroughly cleaning your interiors, you must use dry cleaning services, which also include interior detailing. Nevertheless, a spot on a seat or dashboard area will undoubtedly be removed during vacuuming the vehicle.

Since we are here with a long-term plan, we are unable to be flexible with our prices. Additionally, since each car requires the same amount of work to clean, we are unable to charge different rates for each customer.
